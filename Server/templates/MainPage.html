Interactive Teaching.

Blue&GO! brings interaction to the lessons with a button click, allowing students to learn while having fun.


How does Blue&GO! work?

With Blue&GO, you will be able to interact with your students during class without having to spend time preparing additional materials. Simply connect our system to your computer and turn on the buzzers, start a game using this web interface, and press a button on your personal controller to start asking questions!

Game Modes

Blue&Go offers three different game modes, that will enable you to bring more interaction to your lessons and attract your students!



Classic
The fastest player to answer right wins the points.


Random
One student will be selected at random from all of the ones that pressed the buzzer.


LifeLines
Your students will have a limited number of lifes. The winner will be the last-man standing.
